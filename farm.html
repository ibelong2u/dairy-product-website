The Farm

Sharon got her first goats while in Colorado in the 1990s, mainly as hiking companions (and for comic relief). In 1999, we bought George's great grandfather's farm established in 1891 and moved to Iowa. The 100 year old bunkhouse was renovated into the milk parlor and processing plant and still retains its historic flavor.  Honey Creek Creamery Inc. officially became certified by the state on August 30, 2010 and is the only Iowa certified goat dairy west of Des Moines. We make fresh chevre every two days and welcome you to experience the goat cheese's mild flavor derived from Loess Hills vegetation and enhanced by fresh herbs and seasonings.
